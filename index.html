About Diane Johnson

Structured Literacy Specialist • Dyslexia Interventionist
25+ Years Experience

Diane Johnson is the founder of A Fluent Future and brings more than 25 years of experience as an elementary educator.

Diane provides specialized reading intervention for students who need explicit, systematic instruction to build strong literacy skills.

She works with families seeking support in dyslexia intervention, phonics, fluency, reading comprehension, and structured literacy, always with a focus on helping each child become a more confident and capable reader.

Diane’s mission is to provide clear, encouraging, evidence-based instruction that helps every child make meaningful progress.
